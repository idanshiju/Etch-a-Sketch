Help!

  • Drag the left knob 'L' to change the grid size
  • Drag up to down → remove cells
  • Drag down to up → add cells
  • Drag the right knob 'R' to change the color
  • Double click 'R' to pick color
  • Click "Pencil" to toggle between color and pencil
  • Click "Clear" to reset the screen
